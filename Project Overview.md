# The-Impact-of-Pet-Ownership-and-Bond-Quality-on-Youth-Mental-Health
## Dataset
- https://figshare.manchester.ac.uk/articles/dataset/Pet_Ownership_and_Self-Harm_in_UK_Youth_Survey_Dataset_on_Bond_Quality_Self-Harm_Behaviours_and_Suicide_Risk/29120819?file=54717860
- from School of Health Science of the university of Manchester
- posted on 2025-05-22
- 169 participants, 49 variables
- among young people aged 16–24 in the UK
## Research question 1: Does pet ownership and the quality of the pet–owner bond influence mental health of youth?
- Overall Descriptive Statistics
- Group Comparison Tests(pet ownership / PSR)
- Correlation Statistics
## Research question 2: What are the mechanisms by which the quality of the pet ownership affects the mental health of youth? 
structural equation modeling framework:
- Ownership & structure → Bond quality
- Bond quality → Mental health dimensions
- Cross-dimension mediation
